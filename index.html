a burger shop coming to los angeles

burgers & the kind of salsa you'd eat with a spoon.

bobos sells three things: burgers, burgers with our salsa on them, and jars of the salsa at the front of the counter so you can take the good stuff home. We're scouting blocks in LA, writing menus on napkins, and arguing about pickles. This page is the napkin.

what we sell

Three product lines, one obsession.

The salsa is the headline. It goes on the burger by default, off the burger if you ask, and into a jar if you want to take it home.

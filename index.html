a burger shop coming to los angeles

burgers, made with the kind of salsa you'd eat with a spoon.

bobos is a small idea getting bigger out loud. One thing on the brain: a great burger, and a salsa good enough to put on the burger — not next to it. We're scouting blocks, writing menus on napkins, and arguing about pickles. This page is the napkin.

the scouting board

Where in LA could bobos live?

We're walking blocks, asking landlords stupid questions, and counting how many people already have a coffee in their hand at 11am. Here's the current shortlist — public, because half of you reading this know these streets better than we do.

front runner

Highland Park · York Blvd

walkable · food-curious · neighborhood-loyal

The kind of street where a small counter with a small menu fits in by tuesday. Rent is no longer cheap, but the foot traffic is real every day, not just weekends.

front runner

Virgil Village · Virgil + Santa Monica

young · spicy-food literate · already a corridor

The kitchens on this stretch already speak our language. A burger joint with serious salsa wouldn't be a translation — it'd be a sibling.

watching

Frogtown · Blake Ave

river-adjacent · slow build · creative weekday lunch crowd

Cheaper rent, a built-in lunch crowd from the studios and offices, and bikes on the river path. Lower ceiling, lower floor.

watching

Echo Park · Sunset east of Alvarado

night energy · tourist-meets-local · loud

Built-in dinner and after-show crowd. The trade is a louder room and louder rent. We'd need a real plan for late-night.

watching

Mar Vista · Venice Blvd

westside · families · sunday-market adjacent

Quieter, steadier. Less buzz, more repeat orders. The kind of place where you build a regulars list in a year.

long shot

Smorgasburg LA · ROW DTLA

sundays only · proof-of-concept · brutal honesty

Not a home — a stress test. Three months of Sundays would tell us more about the menu than three months of focus groups.

Know a block we should be walking? Know a landlord? Know someone who already does the best salsa in their neighborhood and doesn't know it yet? tell us.
